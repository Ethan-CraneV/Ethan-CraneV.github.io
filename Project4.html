During my time in high-school I founded and led a couple of robotics teams to the district-wide competition. Our school came in last for both, but it was still a learning experience.

These things were built to complete a predetermined task as outlined by the competition each year. The kits were a great resource for introducing students to robotics. Often there were constraints placed on the robots such as size restrictions or weight limits and that would force the competing teams to create creative solutions to the competition objectives. I'm thankful our school had this resource available to introduce me to the world of electromechanical systems.
